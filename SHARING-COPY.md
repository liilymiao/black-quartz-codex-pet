## 中文长版

我把 Black Quartz 做成了一个 Codex Companion，但没有给她添加猫、狼或乌鸦的身体。她从始至终都是一种几何协议：两枚细长的烟黑晶体围绕隐藏的蓝色档案核心悬浮。没有任务时，她近乎闭合；处理任务时，中缝会展开内嵌结构与权限刺；完成后再次收束。她没有眼睛，观察方向由核心孔径在中缝中的位置表达。这个版本包含九种工作状态、16 个方向、透明 v2 图集与完整 QA。

## English short version

Black Quartz is a geometric protocol Companion for Codex: two nearly sealed smoky-black crystal prisms surrounding a hidden blue archive core. She remains quiet at rest, unfolds attached permission structures while processing, and seals again when work is complete. Includes nine animation states and sixteen look directions.

## English long version

I turned Black Quartz into a Codex Companion without giving her the body of a cat, wolf, raven, or humanoid. She remains a geometric protocol from beginning to end: two elongated smoky-black crystal prisms suspended around a concealed blue archive core. At rest she is nearly sealed; while processing, embedded permission structures unfold from the seam; when the task is complete, the monolith closes again. She has no eyes—attention is expressed by the position of the archive aperture inside the seam. This release includes nine work states, sixteen directions, a transparent v2 atlas, and full QA.
