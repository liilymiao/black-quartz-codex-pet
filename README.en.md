# Black Quartz — Codex Companion v2

Black Quartz is an unofficial fan-made desktop Companion for Codex. She is a quiet protocol entity. Her body consists of two elongated, nearly solid smoky-black crystal prisms. The facets absorb most light and retain only restrained lead-grey reflections. A tiny cold-blue archive core is concealed inside the central seam. At rest, the prisms nearly seal into a floating black double-point. During processing, attached permission structures unfold from the seam. Her sixteen look directions are expressed by the position of the blue aperture inside the seam.

## Visual language

- Smoky black quartz, obsidian glass, deep blue-black, lead-grey reflections
- Cold-blue archive core, narrow seam, restrained refraction
- Science fiction, archive protocol, permissions, order, silence

## Included animation

This release follows the Codex Pet v2 8×11 atlas contract:

- Nine standard states: idle, move right, move left, acknowledge, jump/transition, failed, waiting, processing, and review
- Sixteen clockwise look directions

## Installation

Copy the two files from `pet/` into:

```text
~/.codex/pets/black-quartz/
├── pet.json
└── spritesheet.webp
```

Restart Codex afterward. If an older version remains cached, switch to another pet and then select Black Quartz again.
